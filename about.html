About Oasis Interior Design

Oasis Interior Design is an informational site created to help beginners feel confident designing their home. It’s a guide of innovation and style in the world of home aesthetics. Specializing in creating serene and sophisticated spaces, Oasis Interior Design seamlessly blends functionality with elegance. The company's approach revolves around transforming interiors into peaceful retreats, embodying a perfect harmony of color, texture, and structure. By doing this, the goal is to take the stress off by making home designing simple, practical, and approachable.

My Mission

My mission is to help beginners grasp interior design styles, apply simple room makeover tips, and create cohesive spaces without feeling overwhelmed. Oasis Interior Design is committed to understanding client needs and preferences, ensuring each project reflects individual tastes within a cohesive design philosophy. This approach combines innovation, style, and attention to detail, blending functionality with elegance to deliver environmentally friendly, personalized homes.

About Me

My name is Brittney Smith, and I’m a student at Ivy Tech Community College. For my website design class, I chose to create a site focused on interior home design. Growing up, my mom and I enjoyed watching Home Town on HGTV. I loved seeing the clients’ joy when they witnessed the transformation of their homes, turning what seemed impossible into reality. This inspired my passion for home design, making it a perfect theme for this project.
