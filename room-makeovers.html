Simple Room Makeovers

These beginner-friendly steps can help you upgrade a room without spending a lot. Start small and build confidence.

Living Room Refresh

  • Add a bigger rug (front legs on at minimum)
  • Layer lighting: overhead + lamp + accent
  • Repeat an accent color in pillows and art
  • Add one large statement piece (mirror or artwork)

Budget tip: Swap pillow covers + add a throw blanket for a fast upgrade.

Bedroom Cozy Upgrade

  • Use layered bedding (neutral base + texture)
  • Add matching bedside lighting
  • Hang art at eye level
  • Use warm bulbs for a soft glow

Budget tip: Matching lamps make the room feel intentional instantly.

This simple table shows a beginner-friendly budget plan for small upgrades.
