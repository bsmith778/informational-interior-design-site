Beginner Interior Design Tips

These are simple, beginner-friendly tips you can try today. Start with one room and one goal.

Need a quick win? Click the button for a random “Tip of the Day.”

How to choose a color scheme

Choosing a color scheme involves understanding various interior design styles. Consider choosing a main neutral, a supporting neutral, and one accent. Repeat the accent in pillows, art, and decor.

How to measure a room

Measuring a room is essential for designing spaces. You want to measure walls, doorways, and walkways. Always compare measurements to furniture dimensions before buying to avoid cramped layouts.

How to mix patterns

Mixing patterns can elevate your style when done correctly. Start by choosing a color palette that ties patterns together, ensuring harmony. For balance, pair large patterns with smaller ones, creating visual interest without overwhelming. Keep a shared color so it feels cohesive.

How to pick the right furniture size

Leave 30–36 inches for walk paths. A rug should be large enough for front legs of furniture to sit on it.

Mistakes to avoid

Rugs too small, art hung too high, and only using overhead lighting are common beginner mistakes.

How to create a cohesive space

Repeat 2–3 colors and 2–3 materials across the room (wood, metal, linen). This makes the space feel designed.
