Interior Design Made Simple for Everyday Homes

Oasis Interior Design is an informational website created to help beginners understand interior design styles, simple room makeover tips, and how to create cohesive spaces.

Design Styles

Modern

Clean lines, neutral colors, and simple furniture.

Minimalist

Clutter-free spaces focused on function and simplicity.

Boho

Layered textures, plants, and warm colors.

Farmhouse

Cozy, rustic elements with soft neutral tones.

Beginner Design Tips

  • Choose paint colors last
  • Measure your room before buying furniture
  • Lighting makes a big difference
  • Use mirrors to make rooms feel larger
